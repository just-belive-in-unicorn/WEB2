About

Teachinder is an innovative platform designed to connect educators and learners in a way that's reminiscent of Tinder's swipe-based matching system. It allows teachers, tutors, and educational professionals to create profiles showcasing their expertise and teaching styles, while students or parents can search for and connect with educators who best meet their learning needs and wants. By facilitating these connections through a user-friendly interface, Teachinder aims to streamline the process of finding and engaging with the right educational resources and professionals.

With over 10,000 qualified teachers and tutors, Teachinder makes it easy to connect with the right educational professionals tailored to your learning goals.Enjoy a user-friendly experience with a 95% satisfaction rate among our users. Our intuitive swipe-based matching system ensures you find educators that fit your needs effortlessly. Connect with the top educators from around the world and access a wealth of knowledge without leaving your home. With a 90% success rate in matching users with highly rated educators, Teachinder is committed to enhancing the learning journey for students of all ages.
